Welcome To Satyam International

Satyam International is a Ludhiana (INDIA) based company was established in 1965, almost 54 years ago, under the able guidance of Sh. Ram Saroop Surjan. And the company is involved in the production of india's most reliable Sewing Machines, Overlock Machines, Bag Closer Machines, Sewing Machine Parts with its business set up in Ludhiana Punjab (India).
